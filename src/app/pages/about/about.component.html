About NG Rome

NG-Rome is a not-for-profit community conference run by a team of volunteers. We are all active members of the tech community, and run or contribute to various free local meetups, workshops, and education initiatives.

NG-Rome is the first biggest Angular international conference in Italy organized by AngularRoma MeetUp group. The community is born by a group of devs and the passion for the framework. We are javascript lovers and enthusiastic developer that use the Angular framework. NG-Rome wants to share know-how and want to inspire developer involving the Angular Core team to talk about Angular during the conference.
