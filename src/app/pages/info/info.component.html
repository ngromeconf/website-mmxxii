7. Transportation

By metro
Get on the B line direction LAURENTINA, drop off at the EUR FERMI stop.
The venue 'Spazio Novecento' is at 650 meters/0.4 miles from the stop.
The metro ticket costs 1,50 one-way.
The trip time from Termini Station is 15 min by metro to reach EUR FERMI, plus 10/15 minutes walking.

 

By bus
From Termini Station get on 714 bus. Drop off at COLOMBO/MARCONI stop.
Then walk 150 meters to Piazza Guglielmo Marconi, one there you will be able to see the venue.
The bus ticket costs 1,50 our. The ticket is valid for 100mins from the time you stamp the ticket on the bus, during this period you can get multiple busses.
In case of getting back at late time there is a night bus (BUS Notturno).
